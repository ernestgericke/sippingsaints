Sipping Saints was born from a radical idea: that wine's greatest gift isn't the alcohol. It's the ritual, the flavour, the pause. We craft de-alcoholized wines of exceptional quality so you can savour every sip with full presence.


Our wines are meticulously de-alcoholized using gentle spinning cone technology, preserving every aromatic compound, every nuance, every note. What remains is wine in its purest, most honest form.

✦ The Visionaries Behind the Brand

Founding Saints

Sipping Saints is a forward-thinking South African venture dedicated to crafting premium de-alcoholised wines that embody the sophistication, richness, and joy of traditional wine without the alcohol. Established in 2025, the company aims to redefine celebratory and lifestyle beverages for the modern, health-conscious consumer.


Inspired by South Africa's diverse culture and botanical heritage, Sipping Saints infuses craftsmanship, elegance and innovation into every varietal. Though still in its early development phase, the brand is building a strong foundation centered on quality, authenticity, and mindful enjoyment.

The Craft of Mindful Sipping

We believe the act of pouring, inhaling, and tasting is itself a meditation. Every bottle is designed to slow you down and heighten awareness. No alcohol required to feel the moment.

Technology and Terroir United
